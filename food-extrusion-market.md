<p><p>Food extrusion is a process used to create a variety of food products. Cold extrusion involves using lower temperatures to push food ingredients through a die to create shapes like pasta or snacks. On the other hand, hot extrusion involves using higher temperatures to cook the food ingredients as they are pushed through the die, resulting in products like cereals or pet foods. Both types of extrusion offer unique advantages and are widely used in the food industry for creating different types of products.</p></p>
