<p><p>Smoke detection systems are divided into three main types: ionization detectors, photoelectric detectors, and other specialized detectors. Ionization detectors are sensitive to small particles produced by fast-flaming fires, while photoelectric detectors are better at detecting slow-smoldering fires. The other market includes multi-sensor detectors that combine both ionization and photoelectric technologies, as well as air-sampling smoke detection systems used in areas where early detection is critical. These diverse types cater to different fire risks and environments, offering varying levels of protection.</p></p>
