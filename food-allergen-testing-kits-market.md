<p><p>Food allergen testing kits market consists of various types such as Polymerase Chain Reaction (PCR)-based, Immunoassay-based/ELISA, and others. PCR-based kits detect allergen DNA in the sample, providing high sensitivity and specificity. Immunoassay-based/ELISA kits detect allergen proteins by using antibodies, offering quick and easy testing. Other kits may utilize different techniques like mass spectroscopy or biosensors. Each type of kit has its own advantages and limitations, catering to the diverse needs of consumers in the food industry.</p></p>
