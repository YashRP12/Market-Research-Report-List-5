There are various types of markets for treating amblyopia, including eye patches, atropine drops, and glasses. Eye patches are commonly used to strengthen the weaker eye by forcing the brain to rely more on the amblyopic eye. Atropine drops are another treatment option that helps blur the vision in the stronger eye, encouraging the weaker eye to work harder. Glasses with special lenses can also be used to correct refractive errors that contribute to amblyopia. Other markets may include vision therapy or surgery in more severe cases.</p></p>
