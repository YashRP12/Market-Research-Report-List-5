<p><p>Ready to use tahini comes in two different types - hulled tahini and unhulled tahini. Hulled tahini is made from sesame seeds that have had the outer husk removed, resulting in a smoother and creamier texture. Unhulled tahini, on the other hand, is made from sesame seeds that still have their husk intact, giving it a slightly more bitter taste and a coarser texture. Both types of tahini are commonly used in cooking and baking to add nutty richness and depth of flavor to dishes.</p></p>
