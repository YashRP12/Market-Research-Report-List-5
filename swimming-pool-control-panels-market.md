<p><p>Swimming pool control panels can be categorized into two types: Fixed control panels and mobile control panels. Fixed control panels are typically mounted in a permanent location and are not intended to be moved. They are often used in more established pool systems. On the other hand, mobile control panels are designed to be easily moved around and can be used for temporary pools or for more flexibility in managing pool systems. Both types of control panels serve the purpose of regulating and monitoring various aspects of the swimming pool.</p></p>
