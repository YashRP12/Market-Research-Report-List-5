<p><p>High-Intensity Focused Ultrasound (HIFU) aesthetics market includes two types - Ultrasound-Guided and MR-Guided. Ultrasound-Guided HIFU involves using ultrasound imaging to guide the treatment accurately to the targeted area. On the other hand, MR-Guided HIFU utilizes magnetic resonance imaging to provide real-time imaging for more precise and controlled delivery of focused ultrasound energy. Both types of HIFU technologies are used in aesthetic treatments for skin tightening, lifting, and wrinkle reduction with minimal downtime and risks.</p></p>
