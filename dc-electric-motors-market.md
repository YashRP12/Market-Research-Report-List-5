<p><ul><li>Brush DC Electric Motor</li><li>Brushless DC Electric Motor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>DC Electric Motors are commonly divided into two main market types based on their design: Brush DC Electric Motors and Brushless DC Electric Motors.</p><p>Brush DC Electric Motors utilize brushes and a commutator to deliver power to the motor's rotor, resulting in lower cost and simpler control compared to their brushless counterparts.</p><p>On the other hand, Brushless DC Electric Motors rely on electronic commutation instead of brushes and commutators, offering higher efficiency, greater reliability, and reduced maintenance requirements, making them ideal for more demanding applications.</p></p>
