<p><p>Espresso machines come in three main types: manually operated, semi-automatic, and fully automatic. Manually operated machines require the user to grind and tamp the coffee grounds, as well as manually control the extraction process. Semi-automatic machines automate the brewing process, but still require the user to monitor and control certain aspects of the extraction. Fully automatic machines take care of the entire brewing process from start to finish with just the push of a button, making them the most convenient option for busy coffee enthusiasts.</p></p>
