<p><p>Alpha-fetoprotein (AFP) testing is a blood test used to screen for certain types of cancer, particularly liver cancer. AFP is a protein produced by the liver and yolk sac of a developing baby during pregnancy, and elevated levels of AFP in the blood can indicate the presence of certain cancers. AFP testing is used primarily in the diagnosis and monitoring of liver cancer, as well as certain germ cell tumors and other cancers.</p><p>The AFP testing market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing prevalence of liver cancer and other cancers worldwide.
