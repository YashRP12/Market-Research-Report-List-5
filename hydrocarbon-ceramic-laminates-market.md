<p><p>Hydrocarbon ceramic laminates are commonly used in the electronics industry for their high heat resistance and flame-retardant properties. The market for these laminates can be divided into three main types - Common FR-4, Mid-Tg FR-4, and High-Tg FR-4. Common FR-4 laminates are suitable for general purpose applications, while Mid-Tg FR-4 laminates offer higher thermal resistance. High-Tg FR-4 laminates are designed for applications requiring even greater heat resistance, making them ideal for high-performance electronics in demanding environments.</p></p>
