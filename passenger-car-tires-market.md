<p><p>Passenger car tires market types include radial tire and bias tyre. Radial tires have cords that run perpendicular to the direction of travel, providing better traction, fuel efficiency, and a smoother ride. They are more common in modern vehicles due to their performance benefits. Bias tyres, on the other hand, have cords that run diagonally across the tire, making them more durable and better suited for off-road and heavy-duty applications. Each type has its own advantages and is chosen based on specific driving needs.</p></p>
