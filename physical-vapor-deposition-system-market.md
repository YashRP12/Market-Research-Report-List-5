<p><p>Physical Vapor Deposition System market includes various types such as Cathodic Arc Vapor Deposition, Electron Beam Vapor Deposition, Sputter Deposition, Ion Implantation, and Pulsed Laser Vapor Deposition. Cathodic Arc Vapor Deposition involves creating a plasma arc to vaporize and deposit coating materials. Electron Beam Vapor Deposition utilizes electron beams to evaporate materials onto a substrate. Sputter Deposition involves bombarding a target material with high-energy ions to create a vapor, which is then deposited onto a substrate. Ion Implantation uses ions to modify the surface properties of materials. Pulsed Laser Vapor Deposition involves using laser pulses to vaporize materials and deposit them onto a substrate.</p></p>
