<p><p>Polyisocyanurate insulation is available in two main types - spray type and foam/board type. Spray type insulation involves applying liquid polyisocyanurate foam to surfaces, allowing it to expand and create a sealant. Foam/board type insulation comes in rigid panels or boards that can be easily cut and installed. Both types offer excellent thermal resistance and are commonly used in construction projects to improve energy efficiency and reduce heating and cooling costs.</p></p>
