<p><p>Handheld thermal scanners are used for detecting temperature variances in various applications. There are three main types of handheld thermal scanners based on the wavelength they detect - Short-Wave Infrared (SWIR), Mid-Wave Infrared (MWIR), and Long-Wave Infrared (LWIR). SWIR scanners are suitable for detecting hot spots, MWIR scanners are used in military and industrial applications, while LWIR scanners are commonly used for medical, industrial, and surveillance purposes due to their ability to detect low levels of infrared radiation.</p></p>
