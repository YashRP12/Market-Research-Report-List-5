<p><p>Carbon block market is divided into two main types: Extruded Type and Compressed Type. Extruded Type carbon blocks are formed by forcing a mixture of carbon and binder through a die to create a solid block. On the other hand, Compressed Type carbon blocks are produced by compressing carbon powder into a solid block using high pressure. Both types of carbon blocks offer high filtration efficiency and are commonly used in the water filtration industry.</p></p>
