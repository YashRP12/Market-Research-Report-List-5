<p><p>Silicone adhesive sheets come in two main market types: single-sided and double-sided. Single-sided silicone adhesive sheets have adhesive on one side only, making them ideal for applications where only one side needs to be bonded. On the other hand, double-sided silicone adhesive sheets have adhesive on both sides, allowing for bonding two surfaces together. Both types offer strong adhesion properties and are commonly used in various industries such as electronics, automotive, and medical devices.</p></p>
