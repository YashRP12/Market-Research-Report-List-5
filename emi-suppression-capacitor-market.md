<p><p>EMI suppression capacitors are categorized into two types: Class X capacitors and Class Y capacitors. Class X capacitors are designed for line-to-line or line-to-neutral applications to provide protection against electromagnetic interference in electronic circuits. On the other hand, Class Y capacitors are intended for line-to-ground applications to suppress electromagnetic interference and reduce the risk of electrical shock. Both types of capacitors play a crucial role in ensuring the smooth operation of electronic devices by minimizing interference and enhancing safety.</p></p>
