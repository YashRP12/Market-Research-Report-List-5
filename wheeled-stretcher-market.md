<p><p>The wheeled stretcher market is divided into three main types: manual, electric, and hydraulic. Manual wheeled stretchers are manually operated and require physical effort to transport patients. Electric wheeled stretchers are powered by electricity, making them easier to operate and maneuver. Hydraulic wheeled stretchers utilize hydraulic systems for raising and lowering the stretcher, providing a smoother and more controlled lifting process. Each type of wheeled stretcher caters to different needs and preferences in the medical industry.</p></p>
