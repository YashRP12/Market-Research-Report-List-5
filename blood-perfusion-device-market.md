<p><p>Blood perfusion devices are used in medical treatments to remove toxins and waste products from the blood. Two main types of materials used in these devices are resin carbon and activated carbon. Resin carbon has a high adsorption capacity due to its porous structure, making it effective in removing impurities from the blood. Activated carbon, on the other hand, has a larger surface area and is more commonly used in blood perfusion devices due to its ability to effectively remove a wide range of toxins and waste products from the blood.</p></p>
