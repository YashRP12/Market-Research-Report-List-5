<p><p>Unified Communications as a Service (UCaaS) can be classified into two main types based on the infrastructure being used: Single Tenant and Multi-Tenant markets. In a Single Tenant market, each organization has its own dedicated instance of the UCaaS platform, providing high levels of customization and security but at a higher cost. On the other hand, in a Multi-Tenant market, multiple organizations share the same instance of the UCaaS platform, offering cost savings and scalability but with less customization and security. Both markets cater to different needs and preferences of organizations seeking UCaaS solutions.</p></p>
