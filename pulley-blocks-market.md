<p><p>Pulley blocks are commonly categorized into two main types: moving pulley and fixed pulley. Moving pulleys have a wheel that rotates and moves along with the load being lifted. Fixed pulleys, on the other hand, remain stationary while the load moves. Both types of pulleys are used in a variety of industries including construction, shipping, and manufacturing to make lifting heavy objects easier and more efficient. They are essential tools for applications requiring mechanical advantage and increased lifting capacity.</p></p>
