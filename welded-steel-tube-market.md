<p><p>Straight seam welded pipe and spiral welded pipe are two types of welded steel tubes in the market. Straight seam welded pipe is produced by welding a long strip of steel in a straight line, while spiral welded pipe is made by forming a coil of steel and welding the edges together in a spiral fashion. Straight seam welded pipe is commonly used in structural and industrial applications, while spiral welded pipe is preferred for conveying fluids and gases over long distances.</p></p>
