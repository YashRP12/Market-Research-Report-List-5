<p><p>LDPE packaging market includes shrink films and stretch films as its two main types. Shrink films are heat-sensitive plastic films that shrink tightly over whatever they are covering when heat is applied, commonly used for packaging products for protection and display purposes. Stretch films are highly stretchable plastic films used to wrap around items for securing and bundling purposes. Both shrink films and stretch films are widely used in various industries for packaging and are essential in ensuring product protection during transportation and storage.</p></p>
