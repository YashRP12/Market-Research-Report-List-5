<p><p>Industrial roller chains are commonly categorized into two main types: single strand and multiple strand chains. Single strand chains consist of a single row of links, making them suitable for applications requiring relatively low power transmission. On the other hand, multiple strand chains consist of two or more rows of links running parallel to each other, offering higher power transmission capacity and greater durability. Each type of chain is designed to meet specific industrial needs and requirements, providing flexibility and reliability in various applications.</p></p>
