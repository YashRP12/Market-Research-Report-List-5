<p><p>The nuclear feed water pump market consists of various types of components such as electric motors, impellers, shafts (rotor), shaft seal packages, bearings, and pump casings. Electric motors are responsible for powering the pump, while impellers help in creating flow within the pump. The shaft (rotor) transfers power from the motor to the impeller, and the shaft seal package ensures that the pump remains leak-proof. Bearings provide support and reduce friction, while the pump casing houses all the components and contains the fluid being pumped.</p></p>
