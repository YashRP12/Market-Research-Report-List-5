<p><ul><li>Analog Output</li><li>Digital Output</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hydraulic brake pressure sensors come in two main types - Analog Output and Digital Output. Analog Output sensors provide a continuous signal that varies in proportion to the pressure applied to the brakes, allowing for precise measurement. On the other hand, Digital Output sensors convert the pressure readings into digital signals for easier integration with electronic control systems. Both types serve the same purpose of accurately gauging brake pressure, but they differ in the way they deliver and process the information.</p></p>
