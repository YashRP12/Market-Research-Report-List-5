<p><p>Handheld welding systems can be categorized into two main types: Ultrasonic Type and Laser Type. Ultrasonic welding systems utilize high-frequency vibrations to join materials together, making them ideal for joining thermoplastics. On the other hand, Laser welding systems use a high-powered laser beam to melt and fuse materials, offering precise and clean welds. Each type of welding system has its own advantages and applications, catering to different needs in industries such as automotive, electronics, and medical devices.</p></p>
