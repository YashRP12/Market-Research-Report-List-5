<p><p>Orris oil is commonly produced through two main methods for the market: steam distillation and leaching. Steam distillation involves passing steam through the orris root to extract the essential oil. This method is preferred for its efficiency and ability to preserve the delicate scent of the orris flower. On the other hand, the leaching method involves soaking the orris root in a solvent to extract the oil. This method is often used when a larger volume of oil is needed and can be more cost-effective for mass production.</p></p>
