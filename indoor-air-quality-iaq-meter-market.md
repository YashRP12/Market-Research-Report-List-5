Portable IAQ meters are handheld devices that can be easily carried from one location to another for on-the-spot monitoring. Stationary IAQ meters are designed for continuous monitoring in a specific area, while fixed IAQ meters are permanently installed in a building for long-term monitoring and analysis of indoor air quality.</p></p>
