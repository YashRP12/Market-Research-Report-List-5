<p><p>VoIP market types include Computer-to-Computer, Computer-to-Phone, and Phone-to-Phone services. Computer-to-Computer refers to voice calls made through computers connected to the internet, often using software like Skype. Computer-to-Phone allows users to make calls from a computer to a traditional phone line. Phone-to-Phone services involve calls made directly between two traditional phone lines, with VoIP technology used to facilitate the communication. These market types cater to different communication needs and offer various ways for users to connect digitally.</p></p>
