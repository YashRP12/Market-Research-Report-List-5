<p><ul><li>Floor Mounted Workstation Cranes</li><li>Ceiling Mounted Workstation Cranes</li><li>Monorail Workstation Cranes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Workstation cranes are commonly classified into three types: floor mounted, ceiling mounted, and monorail. </p><p>Floor mounted workstation cranes are installed on the ground and are ideal for areas with limited overhead space. Ceiling mounted workstation cranes are attached to the ceiling, offering maximum floor space utilization and flexibility in movement. Monorail workstation cranes consist of a single elevated track for moving loads along a fixed path, making them suitable for linear assembly lines or repetitive tasks. Each type offers unique benefits and applications in industrial settings.</p></p>
