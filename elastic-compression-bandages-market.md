<p><p>Elastic compression bandages can be classified into two main types: dynamic and static. Dynamic compression bandages apply variable pressure as the wearer moves, providing tailored support based on activity levels. This type is often used in sports and rehabilitation settings. On the other hand, static compression bandages maintain a consistent level of pressure regardless of movement, making them suitable for conditions like lymphedema or venous insufficiency. Both types cater to different needs and preferences in the market for elastic compression bandages.</p></p>
