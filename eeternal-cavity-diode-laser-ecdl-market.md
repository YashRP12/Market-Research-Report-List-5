Mode-locked external-cavity diode lasers, on the other hand, are lasers that produce ultra-short pulses of light, enabling them to be used in applications such as telecommunications and high-speed measurements. Both types of lasers are popular in the market for their versatility and precision in delivering specific laser outputs for different applications.</p></p>
