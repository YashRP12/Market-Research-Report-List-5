<p><p>Vegetable dryers come in various types, including conduction type, convection type, radiation type, and others. Conduction type dryers transfer heat directly to the vegetables through direct contact with a heated surface. Convection type dryers use hot air to remove moisture from the vegetables. Radiation type dryers use infrared radiation to heat and dry the vegetables. Other types of vegetable dryers incorporate different drying methods or a combination of conduction, convection, and radiation to efficiently dry vegetables.</p></p>
