<p><p>Fused quartz crucibles can be classified into two main types based on their appearance: clear and clearless. Clear fused quartz crucibles have a smooth and transparent surface, allowing for easy observation of the materials inside during processes such as melting and solidification. On the other hand, clearless fused quartz crucibles have a more opaque or frosted appearance, making it difficult to see the materials inside. Both types of crucibles have their own unique advantages and are used in various industrial applications based on their properties.</p></p>
