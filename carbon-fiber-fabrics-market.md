<p><p>Carbon fiber fabrics market can be classified into two main types: twill fiber fabrics and plain fiber fabrics. On the other hand, plain fiber fabrics have a simple over-under weave pattern, offering a more uniform and smooth finish. Both types of carbon fiber fabrics are widely used in various industries such as automotive, aerospace, and sports equipment due to their lightweight, high strength, and stiffness properties.</p></p>
