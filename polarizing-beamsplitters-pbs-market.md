There are two main types of PBS on the market - Polarizing Plate Beamsplitters and Polarizing Cube Beamsplitters. Polarizing Plate Beamsplitters consist of a thin film polarizer and a glass substrate, while Polarizing Cube Beamsplitters use a combination of two prisms and a thin film polarizer. Other types of PBS include wire grid polarizers and coated prism polarizers, offering different advantages and applications in various industries.</p></p>
