<p><ul><li>Welding Strapping Machine</li><li>Fastening Strapping Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Semi-automatic strapping machines are commonly classified into two main types: welding strapping machines and fastening strapping machines. </p><p>Welding strapping machines use heat to weld the ends of the strap together, creating a secure bond. This type of machine is ideal for heavy-duty applications where a strong seal is required. </p><p>On the other hand, fastening strapping machines use mechanical fasteners to secure the strap in place. These machines are typically more cost-effective and are suitable for lighter applications where a less secure seal is acceptable.</p></p>
