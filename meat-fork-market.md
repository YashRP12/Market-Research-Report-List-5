<p><p>Meat forks come in two main types: curved and straight. Curved meat forks are designed with a slightly bent tine or prong, which helps to secure and lift meat more easily when cooking or serving. They are ideal for turning and flipping meat on the grill or in a skillet. Straight meat forks have tines that are completely straight and are better suited for piercing and holding meat in place while cutting or carving. Both types offer stability and precision when handling meat, making them essential tools for any kitchen or grilling enthusiast.</p></p>
