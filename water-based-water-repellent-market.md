<p><strong>The Water Based Water Repellent Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Combining Type</li><li>Non-combining Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Water-based water repellents can be categorized into two main types: combining and non-combining types. Combining type water repellents create a film on the surface of the material, forming a barrier that repels water. Non-combining type water repellents, on the other hand, penetrate the material and repel water by changing its surface tension. Both types offer effective water repellency, but achieve it through different mechanisms, catering to various needs and preferences in the market.</p></p>
