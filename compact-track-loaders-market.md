<p><ul><li>Tired Vertical</li><li>Tracked Vertical</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Compact track loaders are commonly divided into two market types based on their undercarriage design: Tired Vertical and Tracked Vertical. Tired Vertical compact track loaders have a design similar to skid steer loaders, with rubber tires for use on various surfaces. Tracked Vertical compact track loaders feature tracks instead of tires, offering better traction and flotation especially on rough terrain. Each market type caters to specific industries and applications, with Tired Vertical models being more versatile and cost-effective, while Tracked Vertical models are preferred for challenging terrains.</p></p>
