Single console boats have a centralized console for the driver and minimal additional features, making them more focused on the fishing experience. Dual console boats have a split design with separate consoles for the driver and passenger, offering more comfort and amenities for recreational boating. Both types cater to different preferences, with single console boats being popular among serious anglers and dual console boats appealing to families and leisure boaters.</p></p>
