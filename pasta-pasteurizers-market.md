Two common types of pasta pasteurizers are compressor pasteurizers and water circulation pasteurizers. Compressor pasteurizers use a compressor system to heat and cool the pasta, while water circulation pasteurizers use a water circulation system to achieve the same goal. Both types of pasta pasteurizers are essential for ensuring that pasta products are free from harmful bacteria and meet food safety standards.</p></p>
