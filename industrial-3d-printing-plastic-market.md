<p><p>Industrial 3D printing plastic market types include powder, filament, and liquid. Powder involves layering powdered material and using a laser to fuse it into a solid object. Filament uses a heated nozzle to melt and extrude plastic filament into a desired shape. Liquid printing involves using photopolymer resins that solidify when exposed to ultraviolet light. Each type offers unique advantages and is suitable for different applications, ranging from rapid prototyping to producing complex geometries and intricate details.</p></p>
