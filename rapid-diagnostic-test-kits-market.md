<p><p>Rapid diagnostic test kits are used for quick detection of various conditions, with different types available in the market. Lateral flow tests involve the flow of sample along a strip for detection. Agglutination assays detect the presence of specific antibodies or antigens through clumping reactions. Immunochromatographic assays use antibodies to detect specific targets on test strips. Immunospot assays involve capturing and detecting immune cells that release certain proteins. Each type offers specific advantages and applications in the rapid diagnostic test market.</p></p>
