<p><ul><li>Volume-controlled ventilation modes</li><li>Pressure-controlled ventilation modes</li><li>Spontaneous breathing (non-invasive)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Volume-controlled ventilation modes in emergency ventilators deliver a preset tidal volume to patients. Pressure-controlled ventilation modes deliver a set amount of pressure to the airways, allowing for better control of oxygenation and ventilation. Spontaneous breathing (non-invasive) market allows patients to breathe on their own with minimal support from the ventilator, reducing the risk of complications associated with invasive ventilation. These different market types cater to various patient conditions and respiratory needs in emergency situations.</p></p>
