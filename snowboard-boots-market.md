<p><p>Snowboard boots are categorized into three main types based on their entry mechanism - front-entry boots, rear-entry boots, and side-entry boots. Front-entry boots are traditional lace-up or boa closure systems located on the front of the boot. Rear-entry boots feature a hinged back panel for easy entry and exit. Side-entry boots typically have a zippered or Velcro closure system on the side of the boot. Each type offers different levels of convenience and performance to cater to the preferences of snowboarders.</p></p>
