<p><p>Power distribution panels are crucial components of electrical systems, serving to regulate and distribute power to various circuits. Low-voltage power distribution panels are designed for systems with voltages below 1kV, while medium-voltage panels are for voltages between 1kV and 69kV. High-voltage panels are used for systems exceeding 69kV. Each type of panel is tailored to specific voltage requirements, ensuring efficient and safe power distribution within a given electrical network. These panels play a vital role in maintaining proper electrical flow and preventing overload or electrical faults.</p></p>
