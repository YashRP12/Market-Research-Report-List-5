<p><p>Sulfur recovery technology encompasses two main market types: the Claus process and tail gas treatment. The Claus process is a widely used method for recovering sulfur from sour gas streams, involving reactions to convert hydrogen sulfide into elemental sulfur. On the other hand, tail gas treatment technologies focus on further increasing sulfur recovery efficiency by treating the remaining sulfur compounds in the gas stream. Both market types play a crucial role in reducing sulfur emissions and meeting environmental regulations in industries such as oil and gas production.</p></p>
