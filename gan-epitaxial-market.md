<p><strong>The GaN Epitaxial Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CVD</li><li>MBE</li></ul></p>
<p><p>GaN epitaxial market is segmented into two main types: Chemical Vapor Deposition (CVD) and Molecular Beam Epitaxy (MBE). CVD involves growing GaN layers on a substrate by introducing gaseous precursors, allowing for high throughput and low cost production. On the other hand, MBE involves depositing GaN layers atom-by-atom using molecular beams, resulting in high purity and precise control over layer thickness. Both techniques are widely used in the semiconductor industry for producing GaN-based devices and optoelectronics.</p></p>
