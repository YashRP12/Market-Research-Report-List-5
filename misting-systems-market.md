<p><p>Misting systems are classified into two main types based on the pressure they operate at: low pressure systems and high pressure systems. Low pressure systems typically operate at pressures below 250 psi and are more affordable and easy to install. On the other hand, high pressure systems operate at pressures above 800 psi and are more effective at producing fine mist for cooling and humidification. High pressure systems are generally more expensive and require professional installation.</p></p>
