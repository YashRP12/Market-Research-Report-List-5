<p><p>COD sensors are used for measuring chemical oxygen demand in water. There are two main types of COD sensors: digital and analog. Digital COD sensors provide digital output signals for easy integration with digital systems and offer higher accuracy and precision. Analog COD sensors, on the other hand, provide analog output signals that can be easily interpreted by analog devices. Both types of sensors are widely used in various industries for monitoring water quality and ensuring environmental compliance.</p></p>
