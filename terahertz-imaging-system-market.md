<p><p>The Terahertz Imaging System market consists of two main types: Passive Terahertz Imaging and Active Terahertz Imaging. Passive Terahertz Imaging systems detect natural terahertz radiation emitted by objects, making them suitable for security and surveillance applications. On the other hand, Active Terahertz Imaging systems emit terahertz radiation themselves and analyze the reflected signals, often used for medical imaging and material characterization. Both types offer unique capabilities and applications in various industries such as healthcare, security, and research.</p></p>
