Three common types include TFT polarizers, TN polarizers, and STN polarizers. TFT polarizers are commonly used in high-resolution displays, providing vibrant colors and sharp images. TN polarizers are cost-effective and suitable for simple displays. STN polarizers offer better viewing angles and contrast ratios compared to TN polarizers.
