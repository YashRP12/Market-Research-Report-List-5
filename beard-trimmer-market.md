<p><p>The market for beard trimmers can be categorized into two main types: reciprocating and rotary. On the other hand, rotary beard trimmers have blades that spin in a circular motion, making it easier to trim hard-to-reach areas. Both types offer different features and designs to cater to the diverse needs of consumers looking for an effective tool to groom and style their facial hair.</p></p>
