<p><ul><li>Polyester Film Capacitors</li><li>Polypropylene Film Capacitors</li><li>PTFE Film Capacitors</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>High voltage film capacitors can be categorized into different types based on the dielectric material used. Polyester film capacitors are known for their low cost and good electrical characteristics, making them suitable for general applications. Polypropylene film capacitors offer high insulation resistance and low dielectric loss, making them ideal for high frequency applications. PTFE film capacitors are known for their high stability and reliability in extreme conditions. Other types of high voltage film capacitors include ceramic, mica, and paper capacitors, each with their own unique properties and applications.</p></p>
