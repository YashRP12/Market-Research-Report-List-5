<p><p>Near-Field Scanning Optical Microscopy (NSOM) is a powerful tool used for studying the optical properties of materials at nanometer scale resolution. NSOM provides high-resolution imaging and spectroscopic capabilities, making it a valuable tool for various applications in research and industry.</p><p>The NSOM market is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years, with a projected CAGR of 13.5% during the forecast period.
