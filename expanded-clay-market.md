<p><p>Expanded Clay, also known as Expanded Clay Aggregate (ECA) or LECA (lightweight expanded clay aggregate), is a lightweight ceramic material with a round shape. In horticulture, it is used in soil mixes and hydroponic systems to improve soil structure and water retention.</p><p>The latest trend in the Expanded Clay Market is the rising adoption of green building practices and sustainable agriculture methods, which are driving the demand for environmentally-friendly materials like Expanded Clay.
