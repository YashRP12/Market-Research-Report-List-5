<p><p>Geochemical analysis market can be classified into two main types: laboratory-based and in-field-based. </p><p>Laboratory-based analysis involves collecting samples in the field and bringing them back to a lab for testing and analysis. This method allows for more precise and detailed analysis, but can be time-consuming and expensive.</p><p>In-field-based analysis, on the other hand, involves conducting analysis directly in the field using portable equipment. This method provides immediate results and is often more cost-effective, but may lack the accuracy and precision of laboratory-based analysis.</p></p>
