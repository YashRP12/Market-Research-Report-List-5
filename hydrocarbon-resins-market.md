<p><strong>The Hydrocarbon Resins Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>C5, Aliphatic Resins</li><li>C9, Aromatic Resins</li><li>C5/C9, Aliphatic/Aromatic Resins</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hydrocarbon resins are classified into different types based on their structure and properties. C5 resins are derived from monomers with five carbon atoms, providing good adhesion and tackiness. C9 resins come from monomers with nine carbon atoms, offering better heat resistance and color stability. C5/C9 resins blend the characteristics of both C5 and C9 resins, while aliphatic resins are saturated hydrocarbon resins with excellent resistance to chemicals and UV light. Aromatic resins are derived from aromatic compounds, providing high thermal stability and compatibility with polymers.</p></p>
