<p><strong>The Industrial Ultrasonic Position Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Discrete Ultrasonic Position Sensor</li><li>Continuous Ultrasonic Position Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Industrial ultrasonic position sensors can be classified into two main types - discrete and continuous sensors. Discrete ultrasonic position sensors provide a binary output signal, indicating a specific position threshold has been reached. These sensors are commonly used for object detection and distance measurement in industrial applications. On the other hand, continuous ultrasonic position sensors provide a continuous output signal that indicates the exact position of an object. These sensors are used for precise positioning and monitoring applications in various industries.</p></p>
