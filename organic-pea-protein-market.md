<p><p>Organic pea protein market can be categorized into three main types: isolates, concentrates, and textured. Pea protein isolates are the purest form with a higher protein content and lower carbohydrate and fat content, making them ideal for supplement and sports nutrition products. Pea protein concentrates have a lower protein content than isolates but contain more fiber and nutrients, making them suitable for food products like plant-based meats. Textured pea protein has a meat-like texture and is commonly used in vegan meat alternatives.</p></p>
