<p><p>Loupe markets can be categorized into various types based on their design and functionality. Through The Lens (TTL) loupes offer a hands-free experience with integrated magnification lenses. Flip Up loupes allow users to easily switch between magnified and normal vision. Galilean loupes provide a compact and lightweight design with good depth of field. Prismatic loupes use prisms to enhance clarity and reduce eyestrain. Each type of loupe caters to different preferences and requirements, offering a range of options for professionals in various fields.</p></p>
