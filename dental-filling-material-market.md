<p><p>Dental filling material is a substance used to restore the function, integrity, and morphology of a damaged tooth. It comes in various forms such as amalgam, composite resin, porcelain, and gold. The material is inserted into the cavity created by decay or trauma, providing the tooth with strength and protection.</p><p>The global dental filling material market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.9% during the forecast period. The key factors driving this growth include the increasing prevalence of dental caries and periodontal diseases, rising geriatric population, and growing awareness about oral health. Technological advancements in dental materials and techniques are also contributing to market growth.</p><p>One of the latest trends in the dental filling material market is the shift towards tooth-colored composite resin fillings, which offer better aesthetics and durability compared to traditional amalgam fillings. Manufacturers are also focusing on developing bioactive and bioresorbable filling materials that promote tooth remineralization and reduce the risk of secondary decay.</p><p>Overall, the dental filling material market is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years, driven by the increasing demand for preventive and restorative dental treatments worldwide.</p></p>
