<p><p>Galvanized Round Steel is available in two main market types: Hot Rolled Galvanized Round Steel and Cold Pull Galvanized Round Steel. </p><p>Hot Rolled Galvanized Round Steel undergoes a process where the steel is heated above its recrystallization temperature and then passed through rollers to achieve the desired shape. </p><p>On the other hand, Cold Pull Galvanized Round steel is processed at room temperature, resulting in a smoother surface finish and tighter dimensional tolerances. Both types are commonly used in construction, manufacturing, and various other industries for their corrosion-resistant properties.</p></p>
