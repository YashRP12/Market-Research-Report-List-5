<p><p>The intracranial neurosurgery market consists of different types of procedures. Craniotomy involves opening the skull to access the brain for surgeries like tumor removal. Craniectomy involves removing a portion of the skull to relieve pressure on the brain, commonly done in cases of traumatic brain injury or stroke. Cranioplasty is the surgical repair or reconstruction of a defect or deformity in the skull. Other types of procedures in this market include endoscopic surgeries and stereotactic procedures for precise targeting of brain lesions.</p></p>
