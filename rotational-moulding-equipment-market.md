<p><p>Rotational Moulding Equipment Market Types are categorized into different machines based on their operating mechanisms. Rock and Roll Machines rock the mould back and forth during the heating process. Shuttle Machines have a shuttle that moves moulds in and out of the heating chamber. Carousel Machines have multiple arms rotating around a central axis to evenly distribute heat. Clamshell Machines open and close like a clamshell to encase the mould. Other types of machines include vertical machines, powder slush casting machines, and custom-built machines catering to specific production needs.</p></p>
