<p><ul><li>Watertight</li><li>Non-Watertight</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Deck hatches are commonly classified into two main types: watertight and non-watertight. Watertight deck hatches are designed to prevent water from entering the vessel, providing protection against flooding and ensuring the safety of the crew and cargo. On the other hand, non-watertight deck hatches are primarily used for access or ventilation purposes and do not provide a seal against water ingress. Both types cater to different needs and requirements in the marine industry, with watertight hatches being essential for vessels operating in harsh maritime conditions.</p></p>
