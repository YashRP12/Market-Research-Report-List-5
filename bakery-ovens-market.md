<p><p>Bakery ovens come in various types, with two popular options being convection ovens and deck ovens. Convection ovens use a fan to circulate hot air, leading to even baking and faster cooking times. They are ideal for baking pastries, cookies, and cakes. On the other hand, deck ovens feature separate baking chambers, typically made of stone, that provide consistent heat distribution for bread and pizza. Both types of ovens are essential for different baking needs in bakeries and food service establishments.</p></p>
