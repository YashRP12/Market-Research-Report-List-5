<p><p>Oxidation hair dye is a type of hair color that permanently changes the color of the hair by oxidizing the dye molecules. This process works by penetrating the hair cuticle and depositing the dye molecules inside the hair shaft, providing longer-lasting results compared to semi-permanent or temporary hair dyes. Oxidation hair dye typically contains an alkaline agent such as ammonia to open up the hair cuticle and allow the dye molecules to enter.</p><p>The oxidation hair dye market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.7% during the forecast period. The market growth can be attributed to the increasing demand for hair coloring products due to changing fashion trends, growing emphasis on personal grooming, and rising disposable income worldwide.
