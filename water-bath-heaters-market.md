<p><p>Water bath heaters are used for heating fluids and gases in various industries. There are two main types: Salt Bath Heaters and Weir Bath Heaters. Salt bath heaters use molten salt as the heating medium, providing efficient and uniform heat transfer. On the other hand, Weir bath heaters use a pumped fluid to transfer heat, creating a more customizable heating process. Both types of water bath heaters are popular choices for heating applications in industries such as oil and gas, chemical, and food processing.</p></p>
