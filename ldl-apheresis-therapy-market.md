Two common types of LDL Apheresis Therapy are Double Filtration and Cascade Filtration. In Double Filtration LDL Apheresis Therapy, blood passes through two filters to remove LDL cholesterol. In Cascade Filtration LDL Apheresis Therapy, blood passes through multiple filters in a series to achieve the same result. Both types of therapy are effective in reducing LDL cholesterol levels and improving cardiovascular health in patients.</p></p>
