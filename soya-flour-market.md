<p><p>Soya flour is available in different types based on its fat content and processing. Natural, or full-fat soya flour contains the highest amount of fat and nutrients. Low-fat soya flour is processed to remove some of the fat content, making it lower in calories. Defatted soya flour is the lowest in fat, as most of the fat is removed during processing. Each type of soya flour offers unique nutritional benefits and can be used in various food products to cater to different dietary needs.</p></p>
