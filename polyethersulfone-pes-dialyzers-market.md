<p><p>Polyethersulfone (PES) Dialyzers are used for blood purification in patients with kidney failure. They are available in two main types based on membrane characteristics: Low Flux and High Flux. Low Flux Membrane dialyzers are designed for standard dialysis treatments, effectively removing waste and excess fluid from the blood. On the other hand, High Flux Membrane dialyzers have larger pores that allow for enhanced clearance of waste products and are often used in patients with high toxin levels or for more intensive dialysis treatments.</p></p>
