<p><p>The manual chainsaw market can be divided into two main types: single side saw and double sided saw. Single side saws have cutting teeth on only one side of the chain, making them suitable for lighter tasks such as pruning and trimming. On the other hand, double sided saws have cutting teeth on both sides, providing more power and efficiency for larger cutting jobs such as felling trees. Both types cater to different needs and preferences of users in the manual chainsaw market.</p></p>
