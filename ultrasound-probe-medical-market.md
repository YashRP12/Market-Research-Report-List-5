The Convex Type probe is best for abdominal and obstetric imaging due to its wide field of view. The Phased Array Type probe is used for cardiac and vascular imaging. The Endocavitary Type probe is designed for imaging inside body cavities. There are also other specialized probes available for specific imaging requirements.</p></p>
