<p><ul><li>Solid-state Laser Welding Machine</li><li>CO2 Laser Welding Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Laser welding systems are categorized into two main types: Solid-state laser welding machines and CO2 laser welding machines. Solid-state laser welding machines use solid-state laser sources, such as fiber or disk lasers, which provide high beam quality and precision for applications requiring fine welding capabilities. On the other hand, CO2 laser welding machines use CO2 gas laser sources, which are more cost-effective and suitable for welding thicker materials. Both types of laser welding machines offer unique advantages and are commonly used in various industries for their specific welding requirements.</p></p>
