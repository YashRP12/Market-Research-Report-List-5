<p><ul><li>Digital Output Optical Proximity Sensors</li><li>Analog Output Optical Proximity Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Optical Proximity Sensors are divided into two main types: Digital Output and Analog Output. Digital Output Optical Proximity Sensors provide a binary signal indicating the presence or absence of an object within a certain range. On the other hand, Analog Output Optical Proximity Sensors offer a continuous signal output that varies depending on the distance of the object. Both types serve different purposes and cater to different applications based on their unique output signals and sensing capabilities.</p></p>
