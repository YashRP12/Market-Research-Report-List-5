<p><p>Printed electronics market offers various printing technologies including inkjet, gravure, screen, flexography, and others. Inkjet technology utilizes tiny droplets of ink to create electronic circuit patterns. Gravure printing involves using engraved cylinders to transfer ink onto the substrate. Screen printing uses a mesh screen to transfer ink onto the substrate. Flexography uses flexible relief plates to transfer ink onto the substrate. Other printing technologies in the market include offset lithography, digital printing, and stencil printing. Each technology offers unique benefits and applications in the field of printed electronics.</p></p>
