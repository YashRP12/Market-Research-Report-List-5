<p><strong>The Emergency Lighting System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Self-Contained Power System</li><li>Central Power System</li><li>Hybrid Power System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Emergency Lighting System market includes three main types of systems: Self-Contained Power System, Central Power System, and Hybrid Power System. The Self-Contained Power System operates independently with batteries located within each individual light fixture. The Central Power System relies on a central battery system to power multiple fixtures connected via wiring. The Hybrid Power System combines elements of both self-contained and central power systems, allowing for flexibility and reliability in emergency lighting operation. Each system offers unique advantages for different applications.</p></p>
