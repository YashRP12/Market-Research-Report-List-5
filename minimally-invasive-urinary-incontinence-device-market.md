TVT involves inserting a mesh tape under the urethra to provide support, while TVTO utilizes a different method of securing the tape through the obturator foramen. Other types of devices may include adjustable slings, midurethral slings, and bulking agents. These devices are designed to treat urinary incontinence effectively with less invasive procedures and quicker recovery times compared to traditional surgeries.</p></p>
